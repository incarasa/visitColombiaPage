Top three activities to do in Colombia

Visit Caño Cristales

Caño Cristales, often called "the River of Five Colors," is a breathtaking natural wonder in Colombia known for its vibrant hues of red, yellow, green, blue, and black that appear between June and November.

Whale watching in Nuqui

Whale watching in Nuquí is a magical experience where travelers embark on small boats into the warm Pacific waters between July and November to witness humpback whales breaching and mothers nursing their calves. An intimate encounter with these majestic creatures in one of Colombia’s most pristine environments.

Dance salsa in Cali

Dancing salsa in Cali is an unforgettable cultural experience in the world’s salsa capital, where music and rhythm fill the air every night. Tourists can take lessons and dance alongside locals in vibrant clubs like La Topa Tolondra, experiencing the fast-paced and energetic “salsa caleña” style.
